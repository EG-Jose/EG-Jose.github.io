iPhone 7 plus long-term review


After 7 years


My iPhone 7 plus has stood the test of time, being released on 2016 and bought on the same year, this machine has been going strong. Though this one has seem its fair share of abuse, being submerged in salt water which it is not rated for, battery being change 3 times in its lifetime. This model was the one that was hated on by hardcores, not supporting a headphone jack is one of the hated parts of this model but now look at what happened, manufacturers now are starting to remove the headphone jack if favor of thr one plug for both headphone and charging

Design and Build Quality:


The iPhone 7 Plus continued Apple's sleek and minimalist design language. The aluminum unibody construction felt premium and sturdy in hand. The introduction of the dual-camera setup on the back added a slight camera bump, but it also brought enhanced photography capabilities, making it a fair trade-off.

The 7 Plus


The iPhone 7 Plus was a solid addition to Apple's lineup, offering a powerful combination of performance, camera capabilities, and a sleek design. While it may not have the latest features found in more recent models, it remained a reliable choice for users looking for a well-rounded smartphone experience. Keep in mind that technology has evolved since its release, and newer models now offer advancements in various aspects, such as camera technology, display quality, and processing power.
