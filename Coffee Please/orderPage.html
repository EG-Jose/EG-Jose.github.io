Regular

A regular cup of coffee, the simplest taste of coffee.


White

A sweet tasting yet creamy cup of coffee.


Black

For the purists out there, only black coffee will do. This is the simplest and most straightforward way the drink is served.


Expresso

When a simple cup of joe isn’t doing the trick, a shot of espresso may hit the spot.


Americano

An iced Americano is a perfect pick-me-up that doesn’t pack too strong a punch. Also great as a hot drink, it’s essentially an espresso shot diluted with water.


Cold Brew

One of the trendier drinks from the past decade is cold brew, which is perfect for those who prepare their coffee at home.


Latte

Typically made using one part espresso and two parts steamed milk. The drink is usually topped with foam that serves as a canvas for impressive latte art.


Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are composed of equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and foam. It’s frothier and can often be topped with cinnamon.
